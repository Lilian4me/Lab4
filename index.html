Scouse

Scouse is the 'national dish' of Liverpool and Merseyside.

Ingredients

  • 450 grams mutton or beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 litre beef stock
  • half a tsp salt
